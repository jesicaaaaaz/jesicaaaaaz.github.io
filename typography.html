Description

This is a typoface design that focuses on the characteristics of both the Mongolian and Manchu characters when handwritten, and preserves the beauty and stretch of the character as it actually appears on the paper. The overall shape of the characters is long and slender, mimicking the vertical writing pattern of the Mongolian and Manchu characters.
